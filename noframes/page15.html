What are our intentions
and how could you be of some use?
 

 
   
   

We always intended to publish the findings of our genealogical research at some point and when the year 2000 arrived, we thought it would be a good time to start. This year marked 300 years since Claes Werry - the ancestor of all Werrys / Werrijs of Kerkrade - was born.

This does not mean at all that our search has come to an end. We still can use more dates and information to make our family tree as complete and up-to-date as possible!

The following data is what we need to know :

• last name: We(r)ry or We(r)rij
  (what is the exact spelling in official documents?)
• forenames (first and middle name / christian names)
• date and place of birth
• date and place of death
• full names of both parents
 
  In case of marriage / living together:
• all data above in reference to the partner
• date and place of (civil) marriage / living together
• full names of the partner's parents
• children, if there are any

We also intend to keep a current directory, so that we can inform our namesakes about interesting findings. So if you have plans to move, or if you have adult children who left the home, or your name or a namesakes address is not yet known by us… please, let us know by e-mail or by filling in the questionnaire.

Anyhow, we would be grateful for any information about the Werry history and we would appreciate it if you let us know that you are interested in publications about the descendants of Claes Werry.

And of course, it goes without saying that we would be very pleased with pictures, mortuary cards, birth announcement cards, paper clippings, anecdotes(!), etc., or if you just want to talk to us about our family tree or history, you are very welcome to contact us.
