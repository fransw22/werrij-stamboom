About our genealogical research
 

 
   
   

In the year 1990, we wrote a letter to all the persons we found in the telephone directory in the region South-Limburg (Nl) with the surname Werrij or Werry. We asked them to help us in our search for descendants of Claes Werry.
We already had been searching for a couple of years at that time.

Since our ancestors where really stay-at-homes (they lived for ages at the same domicile: Kerkrade), it was not very difficult to make a genealogical register that went back to the year 1700. It was a register of the male line, which means that only the father's father is mentioned, and so on.

We decided to widen our search, and look for the relationship between all persons with the surname Werry or Werrij.
We already presumed that all Werrys / Werrijs from Kerkrade (and probably even all the South-Limburgian Werrys / Werrijs) are descendants of the same ancestor: the so-called "goat-rider"
Claes Werry, who was hung at Kerkrade in the year 1743, born as Nicolaus de Werri at Schaesberg in the year 1700.

Of course we wanted to be sure. But to prove that all Werrys from Kerkrade are related to each other - in other words: that they are descendants of Claes Werry - we needed current data.
All the data we had access to, came from Municipal or State Archives, and unfortunately documents after 1940 are not open to the public.
So, that's why we asked all the Werrys / Werrijs for their co-operation. In exchange for data, we would give them a printout of their family tree.

Many people responded to our request, and it even gave us some nice connections. Some people were very kind and gave us pictures, mortuary cards and birth announcement cards.

Eventually, we gave our genealogical research a rest, because our children were born: Annemarie in 1989, Louise in 1992 and Thijs in 1995. But now, we again eagerly continue our search.
